Privacy Policy for Extension Manager

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Extension Manager collects, uses, and discloses information, and what choices you have with respect to the information.

Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect personal data.

Extension Manager is a tool for managing your installed Chrome extensions.

Extension Management

  • The extension reads the list of your installed extensions and apps to display them in a dashboard.
  • This information is used solely within the extension to allow you to enable, disable, or uninstall items.
  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your list of extensions to any external server.

Permissions Usage

The extension requests the following permissions for functionality purposes only:

  • management: Required to access the list of your installed extensions and perform actions like enabling, disabling, or uninstalling them.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
