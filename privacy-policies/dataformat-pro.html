Privacy Policy for DataFormat Pro

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how DataFormat Pro collects, uses, and discloses information, and what choices you have with respect to the information.

Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect personal data.

DataFormat Pro operates locally on your device to format and validate data (JSON, XML).

Local Processing

  • All data formatting and validation happens entirely within your browser.
  • We do not transmit your data to any external servers.
  • We do not collect or store the content you format.

Permissions Usage

The extension requests the following permissions for functionality purposes only:

  • storage: Used to save your preference settings locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

Contact Us
