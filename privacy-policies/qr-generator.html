Privacy Policy for QR Code Generator

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the QR Code Generator Chrome extension collects, uses, and discloses information, and what choices you have with respect to the information.

Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect personal data.

The QR Code Generator operates primarily to convert your input text or URL into a QR code.

External Service Disclosure

To generate QR codes efficiently, this extension uses the public API provided by api.qrserver.com.

  • When you request a QR code, the text or URL you input is sent to https://api.qrserver.com.
  • This data is used solely for the purpose of generating the QR code image.
  • We do not store, log, or share this data with any other third parties.

Permissions Usage

The extension requests the following permissions for functionality purposes only:

  • clipboardWrite: Used to allow you to copy the generated QR code image or link directly to your clipboard.
  • https://api.qrserver.com/: Required to communicate with the QR code generation service.

Third-Party Services

Apart from api.qrserver.com for image generation, this extension does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
