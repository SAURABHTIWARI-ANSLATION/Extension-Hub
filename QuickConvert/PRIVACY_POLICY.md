# Privacy Policy for QuickConvert - Image PDF Tools

## Introduction
This Privacy Policy describes how **QuickConvert - Image PDF Tools** collects, uses, and discloses information, and what choices you have with respect to the information.

## Data Collection and Usage
**We do not collect personal data.**

**QuickConvert** is designed to work **offline** and process your files locally on your device.

### Local Processing
- All image and PDF processing (conversion, compression, cropping, etc.) is performed entirely within your browser using WebAssembly and JavaScript.
- Your files are **never** uploaded to any server.
- We do **not** collect, store, or transmit any of your files or personal information.

## Permissions Usage
The extension requests the following permissions for functionality purposes only:

- **storage**: Used to save your user preferences (such as theme settings or default conversion formats) locally on your device.

## Third-Party Services
This extension does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services. It operates independently without requiring an internet connection for its core features.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

## Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
